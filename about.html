Spotify Music

Spotify music is a platform where you can listen or enjoy our free musics whenever you want. It provides you different different songs you can choose anyone and listen. There is a creative background image in it. There are three buttons and one slider in the site.

Next Button : This button is used to go on next song.

Previous Button: This button is used to go on next page.

Play Button : It is used to play or pause the song.

Slide Bar : It is used to skip or go back in song.
